Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Overview

SwiftDocs is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We Don't Collect Your Files

SwiftDocs is a frontend-only application. All document processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to any of your documents or files.

Information We May Collect

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for analytics and crash reporting purposes.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience, such as which features are used most frequently.

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Enhance user experience and add relevant features

Data Storage and Security

Your files are processed and stored only on your device. We do not have servers that store your documents. Any files you create, convert, or modify remain under your complete control on your device.

Third-Party Services

SwiftDocs may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect anonymous usage data. We ensure that these services comply with privacy standards and do not have access to your documents.

Children's Privacy

SwiftDocs does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require account creation.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the app without creating an account
  • Keep all your files on your device
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which removes all app data from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
